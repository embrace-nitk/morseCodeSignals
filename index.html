EXPLORING HAM RADIO: UNDERSTANDING HAM SIGNALS



Objective:


To familiarize individuals with the use of HAM Signals in HAM radio communication, and its importance in ensuring safety during radio operations.



Description:


HAM Signals, in the context of HAM radio, is a crucial element for enhancing the safety. It involves the study of different types of signals like distress signal, urgent signal, safety signal, and test signal. The use of phonetics in HAM radio serves to ensure safety.
